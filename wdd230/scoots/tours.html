Mayan Ruins

Created close 2,000 years ago, several Mayan Ruins line the Yucatan Peninsula. It is unknown what happened to the Mayas, but it is possible to visit their former cities. These include:

  • Chicken Itza
  • Coba
  • Ek Balam
  • El Cedral
  • San Gervasio
  • Tulum

Scuba Diving

As an underwater national park, Chankanaab National Park, provides gorgeous views and underwater experiences. Guided tours are available. Guides will take you deep to the ocean floor where you will witness beautiful coral and underwater sea life.
