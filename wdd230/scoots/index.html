Scooters

Our scooters provide a fun and exciting way to explore the island. They are street legal and can be used to visit every type of destination. They are safest to drive when the weather is warm and dry. Depending on the model, each scooter can carry between one and two people. A group of people can invest in multiple scooters and drive to destinations together.

ATVs

Take a ride in one of our ATVs with a group of friends for a group adventure! They are the perfect back-country and off-road exploration. They can handle various types of terrains very well. While not as compact as our scooters, they are an excellent option for those that like to have four wheels on the ground!

Jeeps

If you are looking for all the benefits of our scooters combined with all ATVs' offroad capabilities, then jeeps are best for you. They are street legal. You can drive them on major roads and highways. With their 4-wheel drive capacity, they also make driving offroad a breeze! Pick one of these up to explore the major highways and all the back-country sites this island has to offer.
