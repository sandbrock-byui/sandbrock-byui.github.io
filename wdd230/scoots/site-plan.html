Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide Scoots customers, before they visit the island, with an informative and easy-to-understand experience with the company’s services, requirements, and rental process. The website will help the customer understand the “fun and carefree attitude” of being on an island, and inspire in them a desire to explore.

Target Audience

The primary audience of this website is people that want to explore the island on their own. They might consider what rentals are available, how the rental process works, and the advantages of renting a vehicle to tour the island.
