Target Audience

The primary audience of this website is people in communities that are interested in information about the weather. They might consider how the weather is impacting their neighborhoods and want to read articles about that.

Personas

Resident Adults

Resident adults of a given community that have an understanding of how to use a web browser on either a mobile device, a tablet, or a personal computer. They can be of any age or educational background.

Visiting Adults

Adults that are visiting a given community that are unfamiliar with their surroundings and want to understand how the weather is going to impact their trip.

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be like today?
  • What is the weather going to be like for the next five days?
  • What news is there about how the community is being impacted by the weather?
  • Will the latest storm prevent my children from attending school?
  • Are there any weather warnings that I should prepare for?
