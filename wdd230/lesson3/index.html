Site Name

The Weather Lounge

Site Purpose

This site's primary purpose is to provide a go-to place for people to view updates on the weather report. It will give the weather forecast for the current day. It will also give the weather forecast for five days. Lastly, it will offer articles containing up-to-date community information to help users understand weather-related news updates.
